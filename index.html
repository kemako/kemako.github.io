LATEST WORK

Breakfast Machine "Loraine"

Leader / Design / ROS

「できたての朝食のいい香りで幸せに目覚める」ために、ロボットが自動で朝食を作ってくれるシステムを製作中。
Todai to Texasを通じて2019年3月にSXSW参加。

We are developing a robotic system which makes breakfast while you're sleeping to provide wonderful mornings.
We won a prize in Todai To Texas and participated in SXSW held in Texas.
